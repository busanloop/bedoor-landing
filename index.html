About BeDoor

BeDoor is a local operator and community builder based in Busan. We operate spaces, grow communities, and help people from around the world connect with the city — through hospitality, local partnerships, and on-the-ground support.

Right now, we participate in the operation of the BIFC2 22F Lounge, a shared space in Busan's financial district. Looking ahead, we're working toward a network of community lounges at Busan's major gateways — Busan Station, Gimhae Airport, and Busan International Passenger Terminal.

Since 2015, we've worked in Busan alongside global partners and communities including Techstars, WeWork, Start2Group, InnoEx, Edtech Asia, and TechSauce.
